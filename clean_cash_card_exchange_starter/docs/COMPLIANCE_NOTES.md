# Compliance Notes

This is not legal advice.

Core topics to review with counsel:
- Money transmitter licensing
- MSB registration
- Prepaid access rules
- KYC/CIP obligations through partners
- AML program design
- OFAC sanctions screening
- SAR escalation workflow
- Consumer protection and unfair/deceptive practices
- Privacy policy and data retention
- GLBA/FTC Safeguards Rule applicability
- State escheat, gift-card, and consumer refund rules
- Chargeback and dispute procedures
